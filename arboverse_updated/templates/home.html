ARBOVERSE: a data-driven platform about arboviruses

Empowering research and public health workers through comprehensive open-source arbovirus data

OUR GOAL

We provide comprehensive knowledge in a friendly and open-access platform to fight against arboviral disease.

Contact us for collaboration

OUR WORK

We deliver an interactive resource integrating knowledge and data visualization about arboviruses in a broad context to better prepare, respond, and mitigate arboviral diseases.

Explore platform

ARBOMAP

Explore interactive high-resolution maps to investigate risk factors in an ecological context.

ARBOCARD

Find information about arboviral vectors for a better understanding of transmission and improve control strategies.

ARBOVECTOR

Access concise information on arboviruses and their diseases to research studies and informed decision-making.

ARBODASH

Track, understand, and combat arboviral diseases through clear data visualizations and interactive charts.

ARBODATA

Accelerate your research with comprehensive arbovirus data connected to vector and ecological information.
